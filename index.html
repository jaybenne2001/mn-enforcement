Key insight

Why enforcement geography is a labor contract output

The spatial distribution of MSP enforcement is not a public safety decision. It is the joint product of seniority bidding under the MELS CBA (troopers pick zones; management cannot override), road geometry (no paved shoulder = enforcement shadow), NHTSA grant targets ($18M/yr shapes citation priorities), and shift-change dead zones (3× daily, ~45 min each).

The Governor, the Legislature, and MSP leadership cannot place a specific trooper at a specific location. The union contract runs beneath all of them.
